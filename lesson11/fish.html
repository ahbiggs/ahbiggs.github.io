Welcome to the Fish Haven weather page, or as we like to call it Fish Heaven! Fish Haven is known for its large variety of fish that are fun to catch in our beautiful lake. We love our town and hope you enjoy your time here and visit often. There is plenty to do here in our special town and we hope that all who enter feel welcomed and loved! Here are some notable facts about Fish Haven, Idaho: The first settlement at Fish Haven was made in 1864. A post office called Fish Haven was established in 1867, and remained in operation until 1962. The community was so named because nearby Bear Lake is a favorite fishing spot.
