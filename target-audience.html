Target Audience

I want my site to attract those who are looking for accurate weather reports, as well as information regarding good activities that they could do in certain weather conditions. I want my website to grab the attention of an audience who enjoys spending time outdoors and doing so safely. I want it to appeal to the eye and also to adventurous spirits.

Personas

Braden Biggs Braden is a banking supervisor, who is also an avid mountain biker. He is in his mid-twenties and loves the outdoors. He values quality time outdoors, no matter what time of year it is. AnDee Wadsworth AnDee is an enthusiastic outdoor and portrait photographer, who intends to be prepared for every situation that may occur during one of her shoots. She relies heavily on technology to update her when the weather may not turn out as planned.

Scenarios

- User needs information on the weather forecast in a particular city they are visiting

- User would like to have access to weather warnings that could be hazardous

- User wants to see options of what to do in a particular city based on the weather

- User owns a home and wants to be sure to know when it will freeze so that he or she can ensure that they unook the hoses from their house

- User is planning an outdoor event and wants the most accurate weather report
